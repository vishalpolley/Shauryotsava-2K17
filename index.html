About Us

Festival of youth is celebrated in I.E.T Lucknow through a sporting event held across 3 days for the youths of not Just this college but across the state. The Annual Sports Fest of IET Lucknow, Shauryotsava , has all the characteristic traits of an sporting gala with the events ticking all the necessary boxes of required rivalries and competing colleagues in a all-in-one bumper package. Shauryotsava offers neither the biggest nor the grandest platform yet just the mere fact that it provides a spectacular adrenaline-filled three days of emotion-packed tussle spread across teams, sports and ofcourse, colleges create an aura ample enough to warrant a special place in our heart.
